What is AI?

Research on artificial intelligence is a diverse field, and it is progressing rapidly. It encompasses anything from online search engines to military weapons. AI can be defined as the simulation of human intelligence in machines, which includes learning, problem-solving, reasoning and perceiving. AI is used in many industries, and its possible applications are endless.

Strong (General) AI

Strong or general artificial intelligence systems are more complex, human-like and include self-driving cars or robotic systems. These systems can handle multiple tasks and adapt to new situations.

Impact on Society

The impact of AI on society is as diverse as AI systems themselves. While AI systems might be beneficial to everyday lives, education and health care, they also affect human employment and raise ethical questions.

Key Areas of Impact:

  • Everyday lives and convenience
  • Education and learning
  • Healthcare and medical research
  • Human employment and job markets
  • Ethical considerations and questions
